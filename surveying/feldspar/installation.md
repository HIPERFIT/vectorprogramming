Obtaining
=========
Feldspar can currently be obtained through two pacages on hackage:
 
  * feldspar-language - http://hackage.haskell.org/package/feldspar-language
  * feldspar-compiler - http://hackage.haskell.org/package/feldspar-compiler
  
Installation
============
Requires some modifications to get to run on GHC 7.4.1, we have sent
the patches to Emil Axelsson and hope they will integrated soon.
